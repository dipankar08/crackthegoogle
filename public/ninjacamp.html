How this course is different from others?

Technical interviews at a big company are not just about finding solutions to a given problem. However, you will be evaluated in multiple interview axis ( at least 4 different axes in the coding interview and 8 different axes in design interview) which gives sufficient signals on how you would perform in the company if you got hired.

There are so many people who gave the right solution for a problem, but ultimately got "No-Hire" and they wonder why they failed. It might be he had not "exceeded the expectations" in all the "interview axis" or there ware NOT sufficient signals which make a "hire" decision. A rule of thumb, the company prefers to give "No-Hire" for potential candidates to reduce the risk of giving "Hire" for bad candidates.

In our session, we will NOT teach you how to solve a problem (There are so many online resources for that). Instead, we will focus on "exceeding expecting" on all interview axis while solving some good technical problem - which leads to a "hire". The sessions would be taken by industry experts who took hundreds of technical interview and knows how to handle the problem in the best way.

The course is about 15 online sessions, where our expert will demonstrate with real interview problems and how to exceed expectations on all interview axis.
